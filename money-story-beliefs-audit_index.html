How to Read Your Scores

Any category with a higher total score shows which patterns and beliefs are more active and more problematic for you right now. These are the ones to prioritise and work on first. A score that is significantly higher than the others points to the belief system that is currently having the greatest impact on your financial decisions, your earning ceiling, and your relationship with money. That is where the most useful work begins.

The Next Step

These patterns were not chosen. They were absorbed from early environments, relationships, and experiences before you had the language or the standing to question them. Seeing them clearly is the beginning of working with them.

The beliefs identified here have a source moment. That moment can be found. When the emotional charge attached to it is updated, the pattern no longer needs managing. It stops running by default.

If you are ready to find out what that looks like for you, the conversation is the first step.

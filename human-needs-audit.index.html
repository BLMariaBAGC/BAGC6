Your Human Needs Audit

Understanding what drives your behaviour
Why this matters in Week 3

Every behaviour has a driver. Most of the patterns that undermine senior professionals at work, the overcommitting, the people-pleasing, the avoidance, the need for control, trace back to one or more fundamental human needs that are not being met. When a need goes unmet, the nervous system finds a way to meet it. That way is often a behaviour you would rather not be running.

This exercise is a diagnostic, not a prescription. Your job is to observe honestly, not to fix. Bring what you notice to your next session.
